Motivation

We want Pupil to proliferate! We want you to use Pupil to empower and inspire whatever you do. Be it academic research, teaching, art or personally motivated projects.

The software is open and the hardware is very accessible and modifiable because we think that you should be more of a member of the Pupil community than a customer of the Pupil product. We encourage the modification of software and hardware in accordance to the open source license.
